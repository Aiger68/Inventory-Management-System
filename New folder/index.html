About the shop

RP Mobiles — your neighbourhood accessory counter, organised end to end.

RP Mobiles stocks tempered glass, back covers, chargers, cables, neckbands, TWS earbuds, speakers, adapters, power banks and memory cards across ten leading brands. This system was built to replace the notebook behind the counter with a real, searchable record of every item in the store.

  • check_circle Every product tagged with a category and brand prefix, ready for barcode‑style IDs.
  • check_circle Daily sales logged by hand, no billing complexity, no customer paperwork.
  • check_circle Low‑stock and out‑of‑stock items surfaced automatically, so nothing sells out unnoticed.
